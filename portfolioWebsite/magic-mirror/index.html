Magic Mirror

The best addition to your smart-home

DISCOVER

Final Year Dissertation Project

A Magic Mirror created using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python, with Electron being used as the framework.


Written from scratch and with no previous knowledge of these languages, the mirror was created for a client and as such, was produced to their exact specifications.


The project helped me discover a love of front-end design and development, and showed me that I wanted a career in that sector.


Currently, the Magic Mirror runs on a Raspberry Pi, and displays the date, time, current weather and weather forecast, a news feed, a feed of different images and a feed of historical facts. It also has Amazon Alexa built in, and uses PIR Sensors and LED Lights to make the project more interactive for the user.
